# Lotto Number Generator Blueprint

## Overview
A high-quality, professional web application that generates 6 unique random numbers between 1 and 45, optimized for Google AdSense approval.

## Project Details
- **Tech Stack:** HTML5, CSS3 (Modern Baseline), Vanilla JavaScript (ES Modules).
- **Design Philosophy:** Clean, responsive, and user-friendly with support for both Light and Dark themes.
- **Key Features:**
    - Unique number generation (1-45).
    - Responsive layout.
    - Theme switching (Light/Dark mode).
    - Partnership Inquiry Form (Formspree).
    - Disqus Comments.
    - Google AdSense Integration (ca-pub-4870116812658624).

## AdSense Approval Optimization (High-Value Content & Completeness)
1.  **Substantial Text Content:** Expand the "About" section to over 1,000 words of original, helpful information about lottery history, mathematics, and global systems to avoid "low-value content" flags.
2.  **FAQ Section:** Add a Frequently Asked Questions section to provide structured, useful information to users.
3.  **Educational Articles:** Include short articles on probability and responsible gaming.
4.  **Zero "Coming Soon" Areas:** Ensure every part of the site is functional and contains complete information.
5.  **Enhanced Navigation:** Ensure a clear site structure that Google's crawlers can easily navigate.
